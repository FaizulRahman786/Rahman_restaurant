Seasonal Ingredients Guide

How to choose fresher produce and build menus around the season

Why Seasonal Matters

Seasonal ingredients are often fresher, better tasting, and more cost-effective due to local availability and shorter storage cycles.

How to Identify Fresh Produce

Look for vibrant color, firm texture, natural aroma, and minimal bruising. Fresh herbs should feel crisp and fragrant.

Plan Menus by Weather

Use lighter, hydrating dishes in summer and richer, warming foods in colder months for better comfort and nutrition.

Storage and Rotation

Store produce by moisture needs, label purchase dates, and rotate older stock first to reduce waste.

Tip: Build one weekly special around market-fresh ingredients to keep the menu dynamic.

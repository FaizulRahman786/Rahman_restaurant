Spice Selection and Health

Choosing and using spices correctly for flavor, aroma, and wellness

Core Kitchen Spices

Turmeric, cumin, coriander, black pepper, and cardamom form a practical base for many Indian dishes.

Whole vs Ground

Whole spices stay fresh longer, while ground spices are convenient. Toast whole spices briefly for stronger aroma before grinding.

When to Add Spices

Add robust spices early in oil or ghee; reserve delicate finishing spices for the end to preserve fragrance.

Storage Matters

Keep spices in airtight containers, away from direct heat and sunlight, to maintain potency and color.

Tip: Build your own house blend in small batches to keep flavor vivid and consistent.

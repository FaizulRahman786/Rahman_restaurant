Vegetarian Protein Rich Dishes

Delicious vegetarian options that are high in protein and full of flavor

Lentils and Legumes

Daal, chana, rajma, and moong offer quality plant protein and fiber. Pairing legumes with rice or roti improves amino acid balance.

Paneer and Dairy-Based Dishes

Paneer curries, grilled tikka, and yogurt-based sides add protein while keeping meals satisfying and versatile.

Smart Add-ons

Roasted seeds, nuts, and sprouts can elevate salads and bowls for extra protein, minerals, and texture.

Meal Composition

For a complete plate, combine one protein source, one grain, and one vegetable side. This improves satiety and nutrient variety.

Tip: Add fresh lemon and herbs at the end to brighten protein-rich dishes naturally.
